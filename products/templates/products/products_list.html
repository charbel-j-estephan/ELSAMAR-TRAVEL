frequently asked questions

Can you help with visa applications? Yes, assisting with visa applications is among the services we offer. Our expertise includes aiding clients in completing visa forms and scheduling appointments.

What are your cancelation and refund policies? Our cancellation and refund policies are closely aligned with those of airlines and hotels. While we strive to secure refundable options, the specific policy depends on the choices made by the customer in terms of pricing and convenience. As a result, each case is handled uniquely.

Do you offer travel insurance? Yes we offer travel insurance. Your travel insurance will be set up for you in advance and you will be provided with the needed documents.

How can I contact you for assistance during my trip? When you need to contact someone during your trip, please reach out to the number provided to you or the designated employee who assisted you during the planning of your trip.
